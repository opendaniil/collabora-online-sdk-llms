Collabora Online uses a WOPI-like protocol to interact with hosts who want to integrate Collabora Online in them.

Refer to [WOPI docs](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/cloud-storage-partner-program/rest/) [https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/cloud-storage-partner-program/rest/] for further details on the protocol’s inspiration.
