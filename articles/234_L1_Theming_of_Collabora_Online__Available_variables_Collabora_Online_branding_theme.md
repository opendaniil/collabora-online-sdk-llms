The variables below are the ones consumed by the Collabora Online branding sheets shipped under `browser/dist/` (`branding.css`, `branding-mobile.css`, `branding-tablet.css`, `branding-unsupported.css`). They drive the chrome around the document — toolbars, notebookbar, status bar, popovers, dialogs and the integrator settings panes.
